Methodology and Sources

uVidNova is a project-finance-grade atlas, not a humanitarian tracker. Everything on this platform is structured around one principle: every number traces to a named published source. No figures are invented by AI.

What uVidNova does differently

Existing damage trackers stop at "X was destroyed." uVidNova continues into the financing layer, producing for each asset:

  • Three reconstruction cost paths (Baseline / Code-compliant / Build-back-better) as low/central/high ranges in USD millions.
  • A defensible capital-stack template (grant %, concessional %, public equity %, private %) keyed to sector and path.
  • Applicable de-risking instruments (MIGA, EBRD Risk Sharing, EU Facility first-loss guarantee).
  • Named precedent comparable projects where they exist in the Ukrainian reconstruction pipeline.

Cost methodology

All cost estimates are computed deterministically by the following formula:

cost = unit_cost × physical_quantity × destruction_factor × regional_multiplier × path_multiplier × contingency

Where:

  • unit_cost — from data/unit_cost_table.json, sourced from RDNA3 / KSE / EBRD case studies. Every row carries its source code and vintage year.
  • physical_quantity — from physical_specs (m², beds, MW, km, etc.). Every value flagged with source provenance: extracted_from_source, estimated_from_photo, pending_data, or modelled.
  • destruction_factor — by damage level: light 0.10–0.25, moderate 0.30–0.55, severe 0.60–0.85, destroyed 0.95–1.10.
  • regional_multiplier — from data/regional_multipliers.json by oblast. Frontline regions carry higher logistics premium.
  • path_multiplier — from data/path_multipliers.json: baseline 1.00×, code-compliant 1.15–1.25×, build-back-better 1.30–1.60×.
  • contingency — 1.15 (15%) for assessed assets, 1.25 for documented-only.

The {low, central, high} triple is generated by walking the low and high ends of each multiplier range. Central is the midpoint. Heritage assets carry a separate 1.8×–3.0× conservation-premium multiplier, applied in a distinct table and not folded into the general formula.

The AI orchestrator (used for classification and narrative generation) never produces numeric output. All numbers come from the deterministic lookup chain above.

Financing structure templates

Capital-stack templates are sourced from EBRD Ukraine case studies, EU Facility programme documents, and the RDNA3 financing annex. Templates are keyed by sector × reconstruction path. Grant, concessional, public equity, and private percentages always sum to 100%.

Anti-hallucination architecture

uVidNova uses the Anthropic Claude API in two stages: classification (structured field extraction from OSINT) and narration (prose generation against a pre-computed payload). The language model never sees a prompt asking it to estimate a cost or produce a number.

A server-side validation gate extracts every numeric token from any generated narrative and checks it against the structured payload. Any unmatched number aborts publication and routes the entry to human review.
