Methodology and Sources

uVidNova is a project-finance-grade atlas, not a humanitarian tracker. Everything on this platform is built around one principle: every number traces to a named published source. No figures are invented by AI.

What uVidNova does differently

Existing damage trackers (KSE "Russia Will Pay," eRecovery/DREAM, Bellingcat, ACLED, UN OCHA) stop at "X was destroyed." uVidNova continues into the financing layer:

"Rebuilding X is a USD 52M project at the baseline path, USD 68M at build-back-better, structured as 55% EU grant / 25% EBRD concessional / 20% municipal equity, MIGA war insurance applies, the asset has been re-damaged twice since 2022, currently in a rebuildable zone — and here is the precedent in Lviv Oblast."

The build is integration of authoritative sources, not invention.

Cost methodology

All cost estimates are computed deterministically by the following formula. The AI orchestrator (used for classification and narrative generation) never produces numeric output.

cost = unit_cost × physical_quantity × destruction_factor
       × regional_multiplier × path_multiplier × contingency
VariableSource
unit_cost data/unit_cost_table.json — sourced from RDNA3, KSE Institute, and EBRD case studies. Every row carries a source code and vintage year.
physical_quantity physical_specs field (m², beds, MW, km, tonnes). Every value is flagged: extracted_from_source, estimated_from_photo, pending_data, or modelled.
destruction_factor By damage level — light: 0.10–0.25 · moderate: 0.30–0.55 · severe: 0.60–0.85 · destroyed: 0.95–1.10
regional_multiplier data/regional_multipliers.json by oblast. Frontline regions carry higher logistics premium; western regions can be lower.
path_multiplier Baseline 1.00× · Code-compliant 1.15–1.25× · Build-back-better 1.30–1.60×
contingency 1.15 (15%) for assessed assets · 1.25 for documented-only

The {low, central, high} triple walks the low and high ends of each multiplier range. Central is the midpoint. Heritage assets carry a separate 1.8×–3.0× conservation-premium multiplier applied in a distinct table.

On every asset detail page, a "Show cost formula and working" panel exposes every input value and the computed result so any reader can trace the arithmetic.

Re-damage tracking

Assets struck more than once are flagged with re_damage_count ≥ 2. This is a material investor-information field, not a weakness to conceal: it affects insurance pricing, schedule risk, and the case for hardened build-back-better designs. Assets with two or more re-damage events are shown with a red indicator on the map and an amber ⚠ flag in the asset list.

Anti-hallucination architecture

The single largest reputational risk to a platform like this is a hallucinated number cited in a serious publication. uVidNova addresses this by treating the LLM as a classifier and narrator, never as an estimator.

  1. Stage 1 — Classification: the LLM extracts structured fields (asset type, physical specs, damage level) from OSINT input. All numeric specs are flagged with source provenance. Any uncertain value defaults to pending_data.
  2. Stage 2 — Retrieval and narration: the server performs all deterministic lookups and computations. The LLM receives the complete structured payload and is instructed to narrate it. It may rephrase but may not introduce numbers absent from the payload.
  3. Validation gate: every numeric token in the generated narrative is extracted via regex and matched against the payload with ±1% tolerance. Any unmatched number aborts publication and routes the entry to human review.

The validation gate has been adversarially tested against 27 hallucination-injection cases across three categories (tokeniser edge cases, narrative injection, scale manipulation). All 27 are caught before publication.

Disclaimer

Cost and financing-structure figures are estimates derived from published unit-cost benchmarks (RDNA3, KSE Institute) and named comparable Ukrainian precedents. They are not guarantees, not procurement quotes, and not a substitute for transaction-level due diligence. Actual project costs will depend on scope, procurement, timing, site conditions, and the political and security environment at the time of execution.

uVidNova is a not-for-profit platform. It does not receive fees from any financing institution, donor, or government body. It has no affiliation with the Ukrainian government, the World Bank, EBRD, or any other institution whose data it cites. Citations do not imply endorsement by those institutions.

Figures are updated periodically. They reflect conditions and published benchmarks as of the last_reviewed date on each asset record. No real-time damage feeds are used.
